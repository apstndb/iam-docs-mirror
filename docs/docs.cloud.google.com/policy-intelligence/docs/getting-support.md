## Get a Google support package

Google Cloud offers different support packages to meet different needs, such as 24/7 coverage, phone support, and access to a technical support manager. For more information, see [Cloud Customer Care](https://docs.cloud.google.com/support) .

## Get support from the community

You can visit the Google Cloud [Slack community](https://googlecloud-community.slack.com/) to discuss Policy Intelligence and other Google Cloud products. If you haven't already joined, [use this form to sign up](https://join.slack.com/t/googlecloud-community/shared_invite/zt-3icuwfdvq-eq_58LqmOoCNc16mpZcaNA) .

## File bugs or feature requests

From any page in the Policy Intelligence documentation click "Send feedback". This will open a feedback form. Your comments will be reviewed by the Policy Intelligence team.
